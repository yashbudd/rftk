Our Mission

Philanthropy on the move

Each year, members from the Georgia Tech and University of Georgia FIJI chapters unite for a relay-style run, carrying the game ball nearly 70 miles between Atlanta and Athens ahead of the historic rivalry game.

🏃

The Relay

Brothers from both chapters pass the game ball hand-to-hand across Georgia, symbolizing unity and commitment to a cause greater than rivalry.

🏥

Children's Healthcare of Atlanta

Every dollar raised supports children and families across Georgia through one of the nation's leading pediatric healthcare systems.

🤝

Community & Corporate Partners

We're seeking financial sponsors, in-kind donations, and community partners to maximize our contribution to CHOA in 2026.
